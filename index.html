About us

The foundation of the Bhavesh Cricket Academy was laid by Bhavesh Sakla with the sole aim of providing quality cricket training, world-class cricketing infrastructure, and opportunity to learn from experts in the field of cricket through a fun and innovative environment.

The Academy, with its state-of-the-art facilities and renowned coaches, promise to provide quality training to budding cricketers world-over. Through our structured coaching programs, regular matches & tournaments and advisory sessions with experts, young talents are chiselled to become skilled cricketers.

Curriculum

We provide 360 degree cricketing skill development & training through our professionally-designed curriculum using cricket cards, encapsulated modules, strength area development planners, while tracking the growth of the student through daily match analytics & providing regular feedback to parents.

centers

Our flagship cricket academy was founded in 23-acre eco-friendly campus at Jhajjar, on the outskirts of Gurgaon, Haryana in the year 2011. Since then, we have established few more centers in Delhi/NCR and other cities in India. We are also associated with Hind Cricket Club (affiliated to DDCA).

tours

Nothing boosts the confidence of a budding cricketer than touring to different places for playing cricket. We provide opportunity to visit not only different playgrounds in India, but also offer international exposure to our students.

tournaments

We provide on-ground competitive experience to our students through regular tournaments being held throughout the year. Further, our indigenous Junior Pitch cricket league provides hands-on exposure to the real cricketing world to children below 14 years.
